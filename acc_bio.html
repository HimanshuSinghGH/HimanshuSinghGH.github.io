Why Accessible Computing ?

Accessible computing teaches us the various means to make our computing soulution accessible to all people regardless of their type of disability or severity of impairment. Therefore, it helps us in becoming responsible sofware developers who build robust solutions addressing the needs of a wider population. Through this course, I came across that various difficulies that some people face in their day-to-day lives about which I had no prior knowledge. This has motivated to carry out a certain degree of research to build accessible tech especially with the use of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
